DO WHAT? write a program using the English language which produces a composition via a set of instructions. Your instructions should be simple enough that they could be executed by any of your classmates within the context of the classroom.
SCREENSHOTS throughout the semester, you will create a collection of images/videos/gifs made with the screenshot or screen recording function on your computer and phone. you will simultaneously use a time tracking software to collect data of your time spent on the computer, these two collections will be combined at the end of the semester.
REPUBLISH A TEXT choose a text whose copyright has expired, or that has a creative commons copyright. republish or process the text through an online service such as YouTube, Flickr, Vimeo, Vine, Instagram, Twitter, Tumblr, Facebook, Google Docs, Second Life, Google Earth, Google Translate, World of Warcraft, Yahoo Answers, Craigslist, Terraclues, Pilgrim (by Are.na), etc.
20 VARIATIONS This project is an iterative exploration of typography through HTML and CSS. You will choose a poem and make 20 variations on that same text. It is not the single representation of the poem that is important, but the series as a whole, which will be contained by a navigation system and homepage.
MICROSITE choose an event, come up with the 'who, what when, where' and then create a save-the-date website providing information about your event. the design should address both desktop and mobile devices, practically and conceptually.
