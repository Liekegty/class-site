SAFE THE LIFE IN THE PACIFIC OCEAN






FUNDRAISER | DECEMBER 11, 3PM






Help the ocean and its inhabitants: join our fundraiser!
On December 11th, 3PM, we will organize a fundraiser for the preservation of sea life in the Pacific Ocean.
Come take pictures in our photo booth, relax in the hot tub, eat a crab burger, and enjoy the live music by the Beach Boys.






Our vision is of a world where our oceans are healthy, properly protected and full of diverse life.
Join on November 11th, and contribute to reducing plastic litter, prevent overfishing, and increase marine protection.






Boats to the event will depart every 15 minutes from Grand Central Platform 14 (boats will go between 2-4PM)
NB: please bring coins, seaweed checks will not be accepted.
